Copart Gate Pass

Aug. 21, 2023

Dear Customers,

Starting 08/21/2023, Copart will be implementing a Gate Pass Pin system. Clients that purchase vehicles using non-V&S Brothers Inc, accounts will have to provide them, Email: [email protected],  To do so, log in to Copart, go to Payments Due, select vehicle(s), click on Send to Transporter and add [email protected],   Please note that without this Gate Pass pin we will not be able to pick up the vehicle and storage will fall on the client.

Thank you!

IAAI fee change schedule

Jun. 30, 2023

Effective: July 3, 2023

Dear Valued Customers,

 IAAI link about fee change: https://www.iaai.com/marketing/standard-iaa-licensed-buyer-fees

There also will be some changes to the following:

Internet Fees: The Internet Fee schedule will adjust according to a vehicle’s sale price. Different fees will be assessed for proxy and live auction bidding.

FedEx Fee: The fee to deliver one more titles via FedEx is changing from $12 to $20 per FedEx label.

Storage Fee: The daily storage fee will be raised by $10. Please contact your local branch for specific rates.

Thank you!
